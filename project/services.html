St. Francis Hospital Services

On behalf of our entire staff, St. Francis Hospital for Animals would like to welcome you to our community and thank you for visiting our website. We appreciate the opportunity to serve your needs and provide high-quality health care to your pets with skill and compassion. Our hospital provide a full range of general medical and surgical services as well as specialized treatments:

  • Internal Medicine
  • Vaccinations
  • Surgery/Orthopaedic
  • Preventative Dentistry
  • Complete Lab Services
  • X-Ray Facilities
  • General Medicine
  • Ultrasound Examinations
  • Bronchoscopy/Endoscopy
  • Allergy Testing
  • Micro Chipping
  • Flea and Parasite Control
  • Prescription Pet Foods
  • Early Morning Drop-Offs
  • Wellness Exams
  • Dermatology
  • Emergency Service

We are committed to practicing veterinary care with a personal approach. We under- stand the delight, joy, and affection that bonds you with your pet, and we work to en- hance and extend that relationship for as long as possible
